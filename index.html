Campus life

Green Valley International School-CAMPUS

Greenvalley is a group of entities established with the aim of redesigning schools of tomorrow to reform our present education system. We work with a single-minded focus to create world-class leaders and innovators through personalised learning plans.

Younger Kids

In the iPlay Program, all children learn Keyboard in music classes. They learn freestyle dance in the early years and slowly move to semi-classical format. They are exposed to multi-sport program(SportyBeans), and in that we teach children the fundamentals of 9 favorite ball sports (1. Football, 2. Basketball, 3. Volleyball, 4. Golf, 5. Tennis, 6. Cricket, 7. Rugby, 8. Hockey, 9. Baseball).

How They Teach

At Greenvalley, we believe that each child is unique and learns in a different way. We are inspired by the guiding philosophy of ‘Personalized Learning’ for future leaders. Personalized Learning Plans (PLP) is a child-centric academic program designed by experienced educators, child psychologists and School leaders of GreenValley International School that aim to do the following. 1.Celebrate every child 2. Let students discover their strength & passion 3. Impart leadership skills 4. Encourage learning at own pace 5. Support different learning styles

Architecture
Architecture

The Greenvalley International School is the first school in Kerala to have this type of architecture, where curriculum and learning spaces are aligned with each other. Stage 1(Preschool - Grade 2) children have manipulative zones, activity or drama zones. Stage 2(Grade 3 - Grade 7) & Stage 3(Grade 8 onwards) children have their own lecture studios, technology zones, learning commons, art studios, design studios and project work areas. Every learning community will have its own media resource library with large number of carefully selected eBooks, videos and projects. Besides these, carefully selected and designed interiors with pleasing colors, child friendly furniture and innovative layouts, an amazing opportunity for your child to grow and flourish is given.

Where its located
Where it is located

Greenvalley International School is nestled in a vast 10 acre rubber plantation on the outskirts of Trivandrum city. The School is built around a unique floor design that supports Personalized Learning & the Learning spaces support 20 different learning modalities. Learning spaces are split into small learning communities and each community is designed for children of different age groups(stages).

Every learner is unique and has different needs. The student's proficiency in different skills is determined through a form, baseline test and interaction with facilitators. The progress is constantly updated.

What's new

  • Goal Setting

    Based on an initial understanding of the child, yearly goals are set for different skills, which gets converted into actionable bi-weekly goals and tasks. These tasks are implemented through one-to-one sessions, customized learning experience and recommendations to parents.

  • Observation

    The observation inventory and assessments provided through the PLP program enable the facilitators to determine the current progress for each of the skills. Various remedial measures are then recommended to improve the skill levels.
